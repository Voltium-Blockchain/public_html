Companies must make a huge amount of money before they can embark on the solar panel business to contribute to the improvement of the environment. However, cryptocurrency can be used to solve this issue by allowing "not so financially buoyant" businesses or people to use cryptocurrency coins to purchase solar panels for their businesses.
